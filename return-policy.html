Return Policy

Guidelines and clarity on product returns and refunds.

Introduction

At MyoGlow® (usa-myoglow.com), we aim to provide a clear and transparent experience for users exploring the MyoGlow® device. As an independent platform that presents product information and redirects users to external purchase pages, we do not directly process orders, payments, or shipments.

However, we understand that return and refund policies are important. This page is designed to help you understand how returns typically work and where to take action if needed.

Check Items on Delivery

If you have purchased the MyoGlow® device through an external website, we recommend:

  • Inspecting the product immediately upon delivery
  • Ensuring the package is complete and undamaged
  • Verifying that the product matches your order

Any issues should be reported directly to the seller or official purchase platform.

General Return & Refund Guidelines

Returns

Return policies are handled by the official seller or website where the purchase was made. In most cases, you can expect:

  • A return window (commonly up to 30–90 days, depending on the seller)
  • The product must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging, accessories, and documentation required
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)

Please note that return eligibility, timelines, and conditions may vary depending on the platform you purchased from.

Refunds

Refund processing is managed by the original seller or payment provider, not usa-myoglow.com. Typically:

  • Returned products are inspected upon receipt
  • Refund approval is based on the seller’s policy
  • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time may vary depending on payment method and provider

Shipping

  • Return shipping costs are generally the responsibility of the customer
  • Shipping fees are typically non-refundable
  • Some sellers may deduct return shipping costs from the refund amount

Always review the shipping and return terms on the purchase website before initiating a return.

Exchanges

For exchanges, you must contact the official seller or support team of the website where you placed your order. Depending on their policy, they may offer:

  • Replacement products
  • Store credit
  • Refund to original payment method

Exceptions to Returns

Return eligibility may be limited in certain cases, including:

  • Discounted or promotional items marked as final sale
  • Products showing signs of use or damage
  • Missing original packaging, accessories, or documentation

How to Initiate a Return

Since usa-myoglow.com does not process orders directly, returns must be initiated through the original purchase platform. Typical steps include:

  • Locate your order confirmation email
  • Contact the seller or support team listed in that email
  • Request return instructions or a return authorization
  • Follow the provided steps to ship the product back

Important Notice

usa-myoglow.com is not the manufacturer, seller, or fulfillment provider of the MyoGlow® device. We do not:

  • Process payments
  • Handle shipping or delivery
  • Manage returns or refunds

All return-related actions must be handled directly with the official seller or website where the purchase was made.
